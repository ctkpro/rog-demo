REPUBLIC OF GAMERS

“Dare to be different!”is the core value in the booming years of e-sports. Genders, skin colors, races, or languages no longer separate us from connecting with each other. Even misunderstood by the mainstream in society, we hold fast to our will. The diversified style of being Vanguard, Fusion, and Rebel is our statement for rejecting doctrinism. By the border between the reality and the virtuality, We gamers prove our self-existence in our own way.
